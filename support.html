Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my account?
Go to your dashboard settings and click on "Verification". You will need to upload a valid ID document.
What are the trading fees?
We charge a flat 0.1% fee on all spot trades. Volume discounts apply for high-frequency traders.
Is my crypto safe?
Yes. We use industry-leading cold storage and encryption. 95% of funds are stored offline.
How do I deposit funds?
Navigate to "Wallet" > "Deposit". You can deposit via bank transfer or transfer crypto from an external wallet.
