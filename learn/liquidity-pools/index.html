What is a liquidity pool and how to use one?

The engine behind AMMs and how liquidity providers earn fees.

Liquidity pools are shared token reserves used by automated market makers to price trades.

Liquidity providers earn a portion of trading fees in exchange for supplying assets.

Risks include impermanent loss and smart contract vulnerabilities, so start small and learn the mechanics.
