Proof-of-work vs. proof-of-stake

Compare security, energy use, and incentives.

Proof-of-work secures networks through computational work, making attacks expensive.

Proof-of-stake uses validators who lock tokens as collateral to propose and confirm blocks.

Each model has trade-offs in energy use, decentralization, and economic security.

  • Energy footprint
  • Validator incentives
  • Network security
